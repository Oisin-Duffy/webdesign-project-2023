Return Policy

Our policy on returns is as follows

At our jersey website, we strive to provide our customers with high-quality products and excellent customer service. If for any reason you are not satisfied with your purchase, we offer a hassle-free return policy. You may return your jersey within 30 days of purchase for a full refund or exchange. The jersey must be in its original condition with all tags attached. Shipping charges are non-refundable, and the customer is responsible for the cost of return shipping. To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team, and we will provide you with further instructions. We aim to process all returns as quickly as possible to ensure your satisfaction with our products and services.
